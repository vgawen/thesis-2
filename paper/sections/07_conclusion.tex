\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

LLM-driven Playwright healing is dangerously close to ``passes but masks the regression'' when deployed without a behavioural oracle. We measured 78.7\% silent false heal in the adversarial limit, with prompt-level abstention contributing exactly zero mitigation. HealReact's L1 substrate makes 80.6\% of real koenig breaks statically reachable; a small local healer answers 77.3\% of reachable cases correctly; two cheap deterministic levers Pareto-improve it further. We read these results as direct empirical motivation for evaluating behavioural-replay validation in future work, and as a falsifiable cross-app generalisation claim gated by anchor-culture indirection.
